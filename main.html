Present Simple

Explanation: The present simple tense is used for habits, routines, and general facts.

Examples:

  • I eat breakfast every morning.
  • She speaks English fluently.
  • The sun rises in the east.
  • My grandfather drives his car.
  • My mother loves me.
  • they walk fast.
  • He walks fast.
  • She eats a lot!

Present Continuous

Explanation: The present continuous tense is used for actions happening now or around the present time.

Examples:

  • I am reading a book right now.
  • They are watching TV at the moment.
  • She is working on the project today.
  • The boy is learning english.
  • My uncle Is teaching english.

Past Simple

Explanation: The past simple tense is used for completed actions in the past.

Examples:

  • He finished his homework yesterday.
  • She didn't go to the party last night.
  • Did you see the movie last week?
  • The boy studied english yesterday.
  • I did my homework last night.
